There isn't much content to share from Volume 3 1⁄2 of the Queen Times because it's almost completely full of Japanese translations of contemporary English articles, so posting them here would be redundant. This volume is marked as a "Coming to Japan Commemorative Issue" but there isn't much inside to support that–instead, extensive info on their first Japanese tour can be found in volume 4.

There is a small blurb at the end by Ikko, which gives an interesting peek into the life of a high school student running a rapidly-growing fanclub:

"Ah! While editing these eight articles, I felt really good. But I can't believe it's so hard to copy stuff written by a person on manuscript paper! It'd be much easier to translate if that weren't the case. Copying stuff over like this for an entire day will make you go crazy! Yes, it's 3:07 A.M. right now!

I was desperate, knowing I wouldn't be able to make the deadline in time if I didn't take it to the printer's tomorrow, but I have school at 9:30 A.M.... so now I'm frazzled, and I'm annoyed and crazy!

(Wasn't I already crazy though??)

I can't forget to thank Mori, head of the Chubu Branch of the F.C., as well as Warner Pioneer.

—Furubayashi
