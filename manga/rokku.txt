<I>Pure Rock Picturebook</i> is an artbook containing illustrations by shoujo mangaka of their favorite rock songs and artists.  It was published in 1978.  The illustrations are stunning, and include songs such as <I>Jumping Jack Flash</i> by The Rolling Stones, <I>Young American</i> by David Bowie, and <I>Baby I Love Your Way</i> by Peter Frampton.  Artists featured include some members of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Year_24_Group">24-gumi</a>, Yasuko Aoike (author of <I>From Eroica with Love</i> and <I>Sons of Eve</i>), as well as <a href="ueto.html">Nachi Mikami</a>, amongst others.
<br>
<br><figure class="float-left img-xsmall">
  <img src="rokkucover.jpeg">
</figure>
<I>Black Queen</i> is the featured Queen illustration, drawn by Tomoko Naka, who incidentally also contributed to <a href="../doujinshi/manken/QueenIII.html">Manken Queen</a>.  A Japanese translation of lyrics to <I>March of the Black Queen</i> is printed on the facing page.

<script>
document.addEventListener("click", function (event) {
  const sidebar = document.querySelector(".sidebar");
  const menuButton = document.querySelector(".menu-button");

  // If sidebar is open AND click is outside both sidebar & button → close it
  if (
    sidebar.classList.contains("open") &&
    !sidebar.contains(event.target) &&
    !menuButton.contains(event.target)
  ) {
    sidebar.classList.remove("open");
  }
});
</script>
