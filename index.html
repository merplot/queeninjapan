Welcome to Queen in Japan

...a little corner of the Internet dedicated to documenting the special relationship between the band Queen and their Japanese fandom.

Here you will find as comprehensive a list of obscure Japanese Queen fan media as could be expected from someone who A. is not Japanese, and B. was not alive during the band's heyday. Translations, manga, doujinshi, fanzines, unofficial fanclubs, lost media, obscure cultural references, and the connection between Queen and the Showa Era (which encompasses 1926 - 1989) will all be explored.

Are you a mega Queen fan who is hungry for any previously-unknown tidbits about the band you can dig up? Are you a moderate Queen fan who is interested in reading up on their activities in Japan? Are you simply someone with an interest in pre-Internet analogue fan media and culture? Or is your hobby researching obscure stuff that not many people care about? This may be the place for you to lose a few hours.
