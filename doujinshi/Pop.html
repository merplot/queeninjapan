This is evidently the final issue of a series called "Plastic Pop", printed in July 1979 and a product of two doujinshi circles: Manken R/Light Way and I.C.C. Mercury. These groups in turn contain many familiar names, some being members of the circle Atomic Rooster. The printing quality of this doujinshi is very high, and there is a (non-Queen-related) pull-out poster inside.

There are several stories and illustrations that are very beautiful but seem to have very little to do with rock music. Queen-centric content includes a ridiculous BL comic involving various rock stars as well as a cute short story called "A Day at the Races" which I'm posting here.
