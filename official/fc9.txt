<h2>Vol. 9 &mdash; Autumn 1977 </h2>

        <figure class="float-right img-small">
  <img src="9/fc9_1.jpeg">
<figcaption><i>They cut their hair!</i></figcaption>
</figure>
The release of <I>News of the World</i> was right around the corner and the Japanese fans were getting antsy, both because of that and because it had been almost two years since Queen had toured the country.  Unfortunately, Queen wouldn't play in Japan again until the Jazz tour in 1979.  However, there is a note written in anticipation of the band returning that reminds the Japanese fans to mind their behavior by not hanging around the airport and hotels as much.  In 1976, the boys were "beaten up" by fans at the airport and Brian lost a shoe.  It was later recovered, but he was reportedly "furious".
<br><br>
Inouye's section is now titled "News of the Queen", and there's a section where fan club staff were given a chance to hear the new record and relay their impressions.
<br><br>
        <figure class="float-left img-small">
  <img src="9/fc9_2.jpeg">

</figure>
