Vol. 2 — Winter 1976

Vol. 2 was released in early 1976 and contains images from the recent UK tour and info about the upcoming second Japanese tour, which was slated for late March - early April. There are several translations of contemporaneous Record Mirror articles into Japanese as well. Perhaps the most interesting feature in this issue is an account of a Queen Christmas Fan Club meetup that occurred on December 20th in Tokyo and December 24th in Osaka. They mention that attendance was "surprisingly low", but one wouldn't know it based on the photos provided. This meetup included a "film concert", where multiple early pieces of footage that are currently considered rare or lost were reportedly screened.

The fan connections pages continued to grow as fans across Japan used this column as a hub for building secondary clubs and circles. The want ads include the following:
